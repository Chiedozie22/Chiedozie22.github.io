Web Design Steps

  • Determine the Intended Audience
    • The colors, images, fonts, and layout should be tailored to the prefrence of your audience...
  • Determine the Goals of the Site
    • Some of the goals of the websites are: to be informative, to create a personal home page, to establish a corporate web presence,....
  • Determine the General Content and Create a Site Map
    • The site map is sometimes called a Storyboard. Common organizational structures for Web sites are: hierarchical, linear, and random.
  • Sketch a Tentative Layout
    • Be consistent in your overall layout - color, typefaces, logo location, navigation bar, etc. Plan your Home Page Layout to aviod scrolling. Plan Content Page Layout to be consistent with easy navigation.
